About

What We Do

As a recycling company, our goal is to assist the country by collecting plastic bottles ranging from the local community to the ocean and melting them, turning the plastic into usable filament.

Problem/Why?

Across urban cities to small rural towns, there is an extensive amount of littering in many different areas ranging from streets and parks to oceans and lakes. This littering can lead to cluttered streets, filthy parks, as well as interfering with ocean life.
